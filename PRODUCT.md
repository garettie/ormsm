# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Operational risk managers, SREs, and safety officers. They use this during routine testing, historical incident review, and risk self-assessments. The context is professional, analytical, and usually focused on data integrity and clear visualization of safety/risk trends.

## Product Purpose
ORMSM (Operational Risk Management System Module) provides real-time monitoring for employee safety (Call Tree) and structured risk assessment (RCSA). It exists to replace fragmented spreadsheets and manual tracking with a unified, data-driven dashboard for organizational resilience.

## Brand Personality
Professional, precise, and reassuring. The tone is expert-led but accessible. If it were three words: Resilient, Transparent, Cohesive.

## Anti-references
- AI slop: generic, over-designed placeholders or incoherent layout patterns.
- Legacy Enterprise: dense, gray, table-heavy interfaces without hierarchy.
- Dark-mode "Gamer" aesthetic: high-contrast neon on black which lacks professional weight.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: Data visualization should prioritize immediate understanding of risk severity.
- **Systemic Cohesion**: Every component (glass panels, chart tooltips, badges) must feel part of a single, intentional system.
- **Reassuring Precision**: Use clean typography and stable layout structures to instill confidence in the underlying data.
- **Actionable Insights**: Dashboards should not just show data, but highlight what requires attention (e.g., severe responses, critical risks).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- High-contrast support for severity-coded information.
- Reduced motion consideration for data visualization transitions.
